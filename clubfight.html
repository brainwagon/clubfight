Available Actions

How to Play

  • Match: Remove all grid cards matching the played card's rank
  • Sum: Click cards that sum to your card's value (A=1, J=11, Q=12, K=13)
  • Any: Click any single grid card
  • Run (needs ≤2 ♥) Remove a sequential run of cards
  • Up/Down (needs ≤2 ♠) Click cards all lower OR all higher than your card
  • Line (needs ≤2 ♦) Click matching rank, then choose row or column

Jokers: Have no suit/rank — usable only with Any or Line

Goal: Clear the grid before running out of clubs!
